Introduction

In this report, we will provide an overview of the key insights derived from an Excel dashboard that has been created based on pizza order data. The dashboard analysis focuses on three main aspects: days, times, and pizza categories. By examining these factors, we aim to gain a better understanding of customer preferences and ordering patterns, ultimately helping to inform decision-making for the pizza business.


Dasboard Explaination

The dashboard analysis reveals several significant trends. Firstly, it is evident that orders are highest on weekends, particularly on Friday and Saturday evenings. This indicates that customers tend to favor pizza as a convenient and enjoyable option for weekend dining. Additionally, when looking at the times of day, we find that there is a surge in orders between 12-1 pm and during the evening hours from 5-8 pm. This information is valuable for optimizing staff scheduling and food preparation during peak hours. Furthermore, in terms of pizza categories, the Classic category stands out as the primary contributor to both sales and total orders. Moreover, when it comes to pizza size, the Large size pizzas are the most popular and contribute significantly to overall sales.



Results

In summary, the Excel dashboard analysis has provided valuable insights into customer behavior and preferences. The weekends, particularly Friday and Saturday evenings, are the peak times for pizza orders. The hours between 12-1 pm and 5-8 pm also witness high order volumes. Classic pizzas and Large-sized pizzas are the top choices among customers, contributing the most to both sales and total orders. Notably, the Classic Deluxe and Chicken pizzas emerge as the best-selling options, generating substantial revenue, while the Brie Carre pizza falls at the bottom in terms of both orders and revenue. These findings can guide strategic decisions such as marketing campaigns, menu adjustments, and staffing arrangements to further enhance the pizza business's performance.
